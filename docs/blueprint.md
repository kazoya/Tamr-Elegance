# **App Name**: Tamr Elegance

## Core Features:

- Product Showcase & Catalog: Browse a detailed catalog of premium dates by type and luxurious packaging options, including high-resolution imagery and product highlights.
- Smart Pricing & Variant Options: Dynamically adjust prices for retail customers versus tiered wholesale buyers, along with various weight and packaging selections, and integrated delivery logic.
- Secure Shopping Cart & Checkout: Seamlessly add items to a shopping cart and complete purchases through a secure Stripe-integrated checkout flow.
- Immersive Storytelling Platform: Experience the brand's heritage, origin, and quality through rich media, visual narratives, and editorial content.
- Order Tracking & History: Customers can view their complete purchase history and track the real-time status of current orders.
- Basic Admin Dashboard: An intuitive panel to manage products, pricing, inventory levels, customer orders, and basic content updates.
- AI-Powered Product Recommendation Tool: An intelligent tool that suggests personalized date products and packaging combinations based on user browsing behavior and purchase history.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated, muted gold-brown (#AC8639), evoking the richness of dates and desert elegance.
- Background color: A subtly warm off-white (#F4F3F0), providing a clean canvas that enhances product visuals without distraction.
- Accent color: A deep, inviting terracotta (#D6705C), used for highlights and interactive elements to create warm contrast.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) for an elegant, high-end, and fashionable feel, reflecting the premium brand. Body font: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for modern readability across both English and Arabic content, providing warmth and clarity.
- Minimalist and clean line-art or subtly filled icons, consistent with a luxury aesthetic to support visual clarity and refinement.
- Visual-first design with generous whitespace and modular sections, optimized for responsiveness across all devices, ensuring an elegant and intuitive user experience.
- Subtle, smooth micro-interactions and transitions using Framer Motion, enhancing user engagement and providing a fluid, premium feel without being distracting.
